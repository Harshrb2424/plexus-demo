Plexus CodeFest 2024

24th-25th Jan

Overview:

Plexus CodeFest 2024 is an exciting opportunity for coding enthusiasts to showcase their skills, collaborate on innovative projects, and compete for exciting prizes. This event is organized by the technical club Plexus, with the aim of fostering a culture of creativity, problem-solving, and collaboration among students.
